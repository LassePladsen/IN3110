Welcome to Klima's documentation!
=================================
Calculates and visualizes the global mean temperatures for each year, plotted by month, 
from the downloaded dataset `'Global Mean Monthly Surface Temperature Estimates' 
<https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/global-temperature-anomalies/mean>`_
and the scraped data `'Global Temperature Anomalies (1880-2023)' 
<https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/climate-at-a-glance/global/time-series/globe/land_ocean/all/1/1880-2023>`_
all from the source `'National Centers for Environmental Information' <https://www.ncei.noaa.gov>`_

Create the webpage by running

.. code-block::

   python3 app.py

Uvicorn will then give you a link in the terminal which you can go to in your browser.
